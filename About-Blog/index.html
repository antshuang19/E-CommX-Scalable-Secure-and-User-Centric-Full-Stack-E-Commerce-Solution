Object Detection

“Fix the cause, not the symptom.” – Steve Maguire

OTD 很少指代特定日期； 它通常指的是一系列日期。 在大多数公司中，OTD 指的是定义为到期日之前（早）X 天和之后（晚）Y 天的日期范围。 一个典型的OTD窗口是提前5天，晚0天（可以表示为-5+0）。 例如，如果某个项目在 6 月 1 日到期，那么如果它在 5 月 27 日至 6 月 1 日之间的任何一天到达，则将被视为准时。OTD 窗口的概念几乎嵌入到所有现代 ERP/MRP 系统中。

Guarantee Goods

Once you choose hope, anything’s possible. Christopher Reeve

A guarantee gives you added protection when you buy good or services but it does not replace your consumer rights. You are entitled to raise a problem about a product for up to six years from the date of buying it regardless of the terms of any guarantee. You can rely on the remedies available to you under law rather than your rights under a guarantee. This means that if you complain about an item that is not fit for purpose, does not match the description, or is not of satisfactory quality, the trader must deal with it. They cannot force you to use the guarantee.
